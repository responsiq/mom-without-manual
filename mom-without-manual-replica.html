My Journey

The old me meets the new me

Entering into this altogether new phase of life—motherhood—I realized there's a lot to learn, a lot to unlearn. This chapter feels different.

I'm now finding my rhythm again, as the mother of a baby boy, for whom I'm his entire world. Through Instagram and YouTube, I share real moments—not the filtered version, but the authentic journey of motherhood.

This space is for everyone navigating motherhood, finding themselves, and celebrating the beautiful mess of it all. No manual needed—just real conversations and shared experiences.
